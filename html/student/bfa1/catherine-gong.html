Combining analog and digital. I tend to like things that look handmade, so I spent many hours feeling limited by how unforgivingly precise the software was, and when I made them by hand, I spent equally as many hours feeling frustrated by minute imperfections. Learning how to combine the two have completely changed my process and finish.
