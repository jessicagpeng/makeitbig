Post Tactile–Deprivation

TThis poster is created for a fictional event “Post Tactile–Deprivation: Recover Connection and Contact”, which is supposed to take place after this coronavirus pandemic is over. The goal of the event is to recover our close connection with others, with the environment and even with ourselves through a series of exhibitions, workshops, lectures and activities that rebuild our fine tactile function.
