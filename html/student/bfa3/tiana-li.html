GirlFend Brand Development

GirlFend is a line of self-defense armor for women and other marginalized individuals who face harassment on the daily. GirlFend exists in a space where conditions are so extreme that women have to resort to high-tech, inconspicuous yet fashionable garments and tools for prevention and protection.
