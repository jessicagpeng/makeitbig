IdentiFind App

IdentiFind is based around the topic of culture issues in traffic sign usability as well as readability. This app acts as an informative app, builds a community for discussion, breaks down the components of traffic sign design, and displays different types of road signs. It essentially provides a guide for travelers traveling to different countries to help identify road signs.
