CalArts T-Shirt Show Promotion Materials

A collaboration with Ethan Östling and Tiana Li, we not only ran the Graphic Design T-Shirt show, but designed all of the promotional materials. We all designed a poster using various “T” puns. Ethan designed flyers to put around the school. Our biggest piece was a giant t-shirt, with the graphic designed by Tiana, and made by me. I sewed two king size sheets together to create the body, and used 4 pillow cases for the sleeves, using red fabric for the trim. I then tiled and ironed on the design, and hung it near the caf using a metal hanger made by an alum.
