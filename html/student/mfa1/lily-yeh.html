Desert Flower

Image making project experimenting with shadows cast by local plants. The initial iterations were done with spray paint before moving to photo paper, final products combined to form patterns representative of the Santa Clarita Valley ecosystem.

TV Channel Branding — DREAM TV

Branding design project for a fictitious television platform based on dreams. Channels include lucid dream “starter” episodes, dream interpretation and interviews with analysts. Hypnotic, dim lighting style embedded in a surreal atmosphere.

Poster — Precision through Abstraction

99 Practice project for designing an event on a full size poster. The theoretic event is inspired by Taiwanese artist Paul Chiang, and is imagined to take place at the Getty center, complete with musical and dance performances, and a Calarts visiting artist workshop.

Title Sequence — Free Guy

Motion graphic project to design the title sequence for an upcoming movie. Guy is an NPC character who gains sentience in his video game to become a main character himself, scenes are designed to represent the character selection windows from a video game and the overall glitchy feeling is combined with a retro video game style.
