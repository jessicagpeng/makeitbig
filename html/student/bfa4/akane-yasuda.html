miir

Miir is a nomadic experience and interface that projects your personalized perennial plant based on your water balance in your body and mood through the help of advanced technology of an hydro tracker. It is nothing more or nothing less than a reflection of yourself. You can not customize the way your plant looks; its form and transformation are purely the reflection of your water balance and mood, as well as general physical information like age, weight, location, and gender. Miir is a virtual oasis for your plant that grows old with you that will assist mental and physical well being without imposing or forcing the user what the correct way of living is.

Technical Collaborator: David Yoo
